# User Experience Improvement Issues for Merchant Onboarding

## Identified Issues

1. **Submission Portal Experience**  
   - User-Friendly Interface: The portal must be intuitive and easy to navigate to reduce user frustration during document submission.  
   - Guidance and Help Resources: Contextual help or tooltips should be provided to assist users in understanding required documents.

2. **Automated Verification Process**  
   - Transparency of Process: Users should be informed about the automated verification process and required documents.
   - Feedback on Verification Status: Real-time feedback or status updates should be provided during the verification process.

3. **Manual Review Queue for Edge Cases**  
   - Clear Communication of Delays: Users must be promptly notified about delays and reasons for manual reviews.
   - Streamlined Manual Review Process: Ensure an efficient manual review process with a way for users to inquire about status.

4. **Email Notifications**  
   - Timely Notifications: Email notifications regarding approval or rejection must be sent promptly after verification.
   - Clarity in Communication: Notifications should clearly explain outcomes and provide next steps for rejected users.

5. **Overall User Journey**  
   - Mapping User Journey: Analyze the entire onboarding journey to identify potential pain points and technical issues.
   - User Feedback Mechanism: Implement a mechanism for users to provide feedback on their onboarding experience for continuous improvement.

## Research-Backed Improvement Issues

- **Increase User Engagement**: Studies show that clear guidance and support in online forms can increase completion rates by up to 30%. Providing tooltips and examples can significantly improve user experience.
- **Reduce Wait Times**: Research indicates that users prefer processes that take less than 24 hours for completion. Implementing automated systems can lead to higher satisfaction levels.
- **Enhance Communication**: Effective communication during the onboarding process can reduce anxiety and improve user trust. Notifications that explain delays and provide clear next steps are essential for a positive experience.
- **Feedback Utilization**: Collecting and analyzing user feedback can lead to targeted improvements and a better understanding of user needs, ultimately resulting in higher satisfaction and retention rates.
