# Activation Metrics Insights

## Summary

After a thorough search and review of the product-specs repository, no existing documentation or definitions related to activation metrics were found. This includes searches for terms such as "activation", "user activation", and "metrics" across various files and directories.

## Proposal

To address this gap, it is proposed to create a new insights document that will comprehensively outline activation metrics definitions relevant to our product context. This document will serve as a foundational reference for tracking and analyzing user activation and related metrics.

### Proposed Plan

1. **Define Activation Metrics:** Clearly define key activation metrics, including their purpose and calculation methods.
2. **Contextual Insights:** Incorporate context from related product specifications and user stories to align metrics with business goals.
3. **Measurement Guidelines:** Provide guidelines on how to measure and report these metrics effectively.
4. **Integration Points:** Identify where these metrics can be integrated within existing systems and processes.
5. **Continuous Improvement:** Outline a process for periodically reviewing and updating the metrics definitions.

This document will be developed collaboratively with product analysts, data teams, and relevant stakeholders to ensure alignment and completeness.

---

This document is the starting point for establishing a clear framework for activation metrics within the product-specs repository.
